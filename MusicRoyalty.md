# Music Catalogue Investment Analyzer

A Python tool that analyzes music artists' **PSR (Performance and Streaming Royalty) rates** alongside their streaming numbers to identify promising investment opportunities in catalogues and albums.

> Simulate what it's like to invest in an artist — explore who's worth backing before the market does.

---

## What It Does

- Pulls and displays artist streaming data
- Analyzes PSR rates to estimate royalty income potential
- Surfaces artists whose catalogue or album rights could be worth acquiring
- Helps anyone curious about music investment think like an A&R or catalogue fund

---
